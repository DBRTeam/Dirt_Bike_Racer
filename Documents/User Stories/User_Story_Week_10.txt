At the end of the week the user will be able to play a fully functional game. 
They will be able to select what language they want the game to be played in. 
They will be able to read instructions, as well as choose to start the game.
Once in the game they will be able to control the dirt bike across a level, and
see exactly how long it takes them to beat the level. Also they will be able 
to experience a crash or win state.  Also once they have beaten all three levels the user
will be able to see that they beat the game.
